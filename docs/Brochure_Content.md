### **Page 3: Zero Hour Management**
**[Section]**: Session Management
**[Headline]**: Matters Raised with Permission

**[Content]**
Zero Hour requires precise time management to accommodate maximum submissions. The system offers a dedicated countdown interface that ensures speakers adhere to strict 3-minute time limits.

**[Visual Element]**
*   *Image*: Zero Hour Screen (Shri C. Sadanandan Master)
*   *Caption*: Figure 2: The Broadcast View shows the speaker's details and a high-visibility countdown timer.

**[Key Features]**
*   **Identity Integration**: Instantly pulls Name, Constituency, and Photo.
*   **Visual Alerts**: Timer changes color (Green -> Orange -> Red) as time expires.
*   **Seat Number Display**: Large display for easy identification.

### **Page 4: Bill Discussion & Time Allocation**
**[Section]**: Legislative Business
**[Headline]**: Complex Bill Discussions

**[Content]**
Unlike standard interventions, Legislative Bills require time allocation based on party strength. The 'Discussions' module is built to handle this complexity automatically.

**[Visual Element]**
*   *Image*: Discussion Screen (Dr. Kalpana Saini / International Passport Bill)
*   *Caption*: Figure 3: Active Bill Discussion showing Member usage vs Party Allocation.

**[Features]**
*   **Dynamic Calculations**: Automatically subtracts current speaker's time from Party's total.
*   **Dual-Timer System**: Simultaneous tracking of "Spoken Time" and "Remaining Party Time".
